The Competition

The American Regional Biology Competition is a national biology competition that tests students on their knowledge in cell structure, interactions between organisms, homeostasis, genetics, and evolution.

Competition rules

In ARBC, there are 3 rounds of competition, for teams of 3-4 people. In the Individual Round, each competitor will complete a timed multiple choice exam testing all basic fields of biology. Each individual will have 60 minutes to complete a set of ___ questions. Each question is worth 1 point, with no points awarded for incorrect or blank answers. Conferring between team members is NOT allowed.

Rounds

Calculators? In the Theoretical Round, each team will be given a set of 10 biology-related passages, and will have to answer __ _____ questions each passage. 60 minutes will be given for each team to complete this round. Cooperation with teammates will be necessary to succeed in this round. In the Research Round, each team will be given 2 published scientific papers to read through. These papers will involve current findings in biology fields. Each team will have 40 minutes to read through the papers and answer ___ questions designed to test comprehension, interpretation, and synthesis. Teams will be given access to the Internet and books to help answer questions. There is a fourth round, the ___ Round, entirely for fun, with no impact on competition standings.
